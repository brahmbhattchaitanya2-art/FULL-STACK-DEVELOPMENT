Shree Ram refers to Lord Rama, a major deity in Hinduism and the seventh incarnation of the god Vishnu. He is the central figure of the ancient Hindu epic, the Ramayana, and is considered an ideal man and an embodiment of dharma. The phrase "Jai Shree Ram" is a popular chant meaning "Hail Lord Ram" or "Victory to Lord Ram".
